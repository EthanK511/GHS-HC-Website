The Hack Club philosophy

We’re at our best when we’re making.

Hack Club is a global nonprofit network of clubs where teens learn to code, design, and build projects together. We meet at lunch on Tuesdays and Thursdays in the Makerspace (042).

Build first, understand while you go

The philosophy is simple: make things, ship them, and learn by creating.

Join the club

Coding is a superpower

Learning to code turns you from a consumer into a creator and makes computers a tool for building.

Make from anywhere

Anyone with a laptop and internet connection can start making real things, wherever they are.

Hack, hack, hack

We want a space at every school where people are making interesting things with code every week.

Start building

Instead of only learning concepts, you begin by shipping a project at every meeting.

Learn as you build

You may not understand everything at first, but you learn by doing and by getting stuck together.

Be part of a community

Hack Club connects you with thousands of young makers who share ideas, help one another, and have fun.
